Our Story

From a small garage to your favorite corner.

"Coffee is more than a beverage; it is a moment of connection."

Founded in 2024, BrewHaven began with a simple mission: to slow down the world, if only for a few minutes. We traveled to the highlands of Ethiopia and the volcanic soils of Guatemala to find farmers who share our passion for quality and sustainability.

Sustainable Sourcing

We believe in fair trade not just as a label, but as a practice. We pay our farmers 20% above market rates to ensure they can invest in their communities and families. Every bean you drink supports a global ecosystem of care.

The Roasting Process

Our master roasters treat every batch like a science experiment and an art project combined. We roast in small batches in our local facility, ensuring that the coffee in your cup was likely green beans just a few days ago.
