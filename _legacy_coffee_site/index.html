Our Story

Welcome to Cozy Cup, where every cup tells a story. We believe that coffee is more than just a drink—it’s a ritual, a moment of pause, and a way to connect.

Founded with a passion for high-quality beans and friendly service, we strive to create a peaceful haven where you can relax, work, or catch up with friends.
